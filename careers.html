We believe in a balanced environment, where positivity, hard work and happy hours coexist. Here, you need to be really great at what you do and have a passion for producing top quality work. If you’re searching for a place that works with the best in the industry and adds value to the business, then select the opportunity that best fits your career path.

EXPERIENCED BARISTAS

We are searching for an experienced, friendly, motivated and enthusiastic baristas to join our growing team. We are seeking team members who possess:

‣ Great work ethic and superior customer service skills
‣ Passion for food and coffee!
‣ Ability to work fast but accurately
‣ Friendly, motivated, and enthusiastic attitude
‣ Ability to multi-task during busy store hours

Job duties will include crafting espresso beverages, smoothies, juices, and basic knowledge of a POS cash register. Additional job description entails cleaning / stocking, etc.
Apply in person at one of our scheduled open interviews, or send resume/work history to jobs@atriumcafe.com ­

FRONT COUNTER POSITIONS

We are looking for enthusiastic counter help! Job duties include order taking at the register, assisting with making espresso/juice/smoothie drinks, lots of smiling, and great customer service skills!
Apply in person at one of our scheduled open interviews, or send resume/work history to jobs@atriumcafe.com

EXPERIENCED LINE COOKS and KITCHEN MANAGERS

We are looking to hire a dynamic individual with the following qualities:

‣ Casual and/or quick-service/line cook experience
‣ Have a solid grasp on kitchen operations
‣ Be able to work fast but accurately
‣ Have a great attitude, enthusiasm and passion for the industry
‣ Have high levels of food quality, hygiene and restaurant standards.

Apply in person at one of our scheduled open interviews, or send resume/work history to jobs@atriumcafe.com
