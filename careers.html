Join Our Team

We believe in a balanced environment, where positivity, hard work and happy hours coexist. Here, you need to be really great at what you do and have a passion for producing top quality work.

Barista

$14.30 - $17.50 Hourly

  • Great work ethic and superior customer service skills
  • Passion for food and coffee!
  • Ability to work fast but accurately
  • Friendly, motivated, and enthusiastic attitude
  • Ability to multi-task during busy store hours

Line Cook

$18.30 - $20.50 Hourly

  • Casual and/or quick-service/line cook experience
  • Have a solid grasp on kitchen operations
  • Be able to work fast but accurately
  • Have a great attitude, enthusiasm and passion for the industry
  • Have high levels of food quality, hygiene and restaurant standards.

Host

$13.00 - $14.50 Hourly

  • Passion for providing extraordinary service
  • The ability to thrive in a fast-paced environment
  • An appetite for learning (and great food)
  • Ability to stand/walk and stay focused and alert for extended periods of time

Manager

$17.00 - $22.00 Hourly

  • Excellent interpersonal skills
  • Strong written and oral communication skills
  • Ability to cope under pressure
  • Good business awareness
  • Ability to both work in and lead a team
  • Problem-solving ability

Apply in person at one of our scheduled interviews, or send resume to jobs@atrium.com
